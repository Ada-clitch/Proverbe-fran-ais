Détails de Proverbes

Happiness is in the little


True happiness doesn't always come from big achievements or grand celebrations. Often, it lives quietly in the simple, everyday moments that we might overlook a gentle breeze, or a warm cup of tea.

Do not be afraid to try


Do not be afraid to try. Every step forward begins with courage. Trying opens doors to growth, learning, and unexpected joy. Even if you stumble, each attempt shapes who you are.

Nothing lasts forever


Nothing lasts forever. Whether it’s pain or joy, everything is temporary. Embrace the good while it lasts, and trust that the hard times will pass too.

It is never too late


It's never too late. Change, growth, and dreams have no expiration date. Every new day offers a fresh start, no matter your age or past.

Life goes on


Life goes on. Even when things feel heavy, the world keeps moving. Time brings healing, new experiences, and renewed strength.

Do not forget to live


Do not forget to live. Amid goals and responsibilities, remember to enjoy the moment. Laugh, breathe, connect, explore. Life isn’t just about surviving—it’s about truly living.
